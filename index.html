Features

Accident Detection

Utilizes advanced algorithms to detect accidents using your phone's sensors.

Automatic Emergency Calls

Automatically places a call to emergency services and sends an alert to your emergency contacts.

Location Sharing

Sends your exact location to help emergency responders reach you quickly.

Health Information Sharing

Shares your critical health information with emergency responders for better care.

Manual Trigger

Allows you to manually trigger an emergency alert if you are in danger.

False Positive Handling

Includes a verification system to reduce false alarms.

How It Works

1. Setup

Enter your emergency contacts and health information.

2. Continuous Monitoring

The app monitors your phone's sensors for signs of an accident.

3. Emergency Response

In case of an accident, the app automatically calls for help and sends your location and health information.
